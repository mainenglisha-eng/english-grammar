Interactive Grammar Table

Grammar Word Meaning Example Practice Here
N
Noun
a person, place, or thing Mary, China, pen
V
Verb
something we do do, read, write
Adj
Adjective
describes a noun good, bad, happy, long
Adv
Adverb
describes a verb slowly, badly
P
Preposition
use it before a noun or pronoun in, on, by, at, through
S
Singular
one noun book, house, child
Pl
Plural
more than one noun books, houses, children
Ph
Phrase
a group of words (not a complete sentence) in a house, at home, an old man
Sen
Sentence
an idea that begins with a capital letter and ends with a period; a sentence usually has a subject and a verb. The man went into the room and closed the door.
Par
Paragraph
one or more sentences about the same topic, beginning on a new line This book has 60 units. Each unit has 2 pages.
Q
Question
a group of words that begin with a capital letter and end with a question mark. What time is it? Do you speak Spanish?
